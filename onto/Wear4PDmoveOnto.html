Abstract

The designed Wear4PDmoveOnto aims to semantically integrate heterogeneous data such as dynamic/stream data (collected from wearables) and static/historic data (stored in health databases), in order to represent personal health knowledge in the form of a KG, supporting health apps’ reasoning capabilities for high-level events recognition (e.g., ‘missing dose’ or ‘patient fall’ event).
